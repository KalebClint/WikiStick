History

I have a couple of siblings that crochet, and both did so before I ever did. One sister much older, and then my younger sister as well. They were probably where I got the main idea of crocheting from, but weren't the reason I ever started. I am a big Brandon Sanderson Fan, he's an auther of many books, and at one point, it was nearing christmas, I saw a really cool and cute crochet pattern from one of his books. It was called a doomslug. I had the idea, and then basically started learning to crochet, for no other reason then for a doomslug.
I crocheted the doomslug for one of my sisters, and my dad, both being Brandon Sanderson fans as well. By that point I had crocheted three doomslugs, One test, and one for my sister and my dad. While on holiday I was talking with another fan, and decided to crochet another doomslug for them as well.
I havn't crocheted much since then at all, a few things here and there. Only like two of them being a non-doomslug. As I actually crocheteed two more doomslugs for other friends who were also Brandon Sanderson fans. Meaning I've made like six of them in total. One other thing I amde for another friend was a crochet of Sir Alexander from Elden ring, it didn't turn out great, but it was cute.
I'm not very good at crocheting overall, I didn't do too much learning before just figuring out how to do that one doomslug pattern, so I can't really do any other patterns very well at all.

Why I like It

I don't mind crocheting, its a nice thing to do when watching a movie or something like that, or when doing nothing else, just listening to music and thinking about things. Its a relaxing thing to do, but never erally did fit me very much, so I never got into a whole ton. However, one of the bigger reasons I like crocheting, is that it comes in handy when you need a present for someone's birthday / christmas, but you are completely broke. (:
