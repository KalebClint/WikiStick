History

My entire family has at least at some point been pretty avid readers, which is a big reason I frist got into reading at all. The first books I read when I was younger would have been the short ones like Zack power, or Beast Quest. But the first proper book I ever read was when my sister got me to read Percy Jackson. I loved it, and have read that series several times over already.

Why I like It

This might be a bit of repetition, but the main reason I loved reading is becouse its a sort of form of living in those stories. I was a very avid reader for a long while, and really enjoyed reading books, finding different stories and characters.

What I do with it

I don't do a lot of reading now anymore, though I am trying to get myself back into reading. Some of my favourite books are those written by Brandon Sanderson, I could likely rant for a while as to why I love his books, they are honestly amazing. Any time any of his books come out I will likely be immeditately getting and reading them within at least a day.
