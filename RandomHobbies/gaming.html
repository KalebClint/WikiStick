Why I like It

I really enjoy gaming, likely for similar reasons to most other people. It does depend on the game, but often times it's simply a nice way to pass time. With some games I enjoy the challenge, others the puzzle parts to it. Some simply the lovely art styles. However, not a sinlge one of my favourite games I loved mostly becouse it was 'challenging' or 'looked nice.' All of the games that I love, all share one thing in common. They have a story. And a good one at that too.
Most everything I love to do in some way has to do with stories, I love movies and books, not just reading and watching, but even writing, 3d art. I enjoy game design not just becouse the coding can be (the wrost the thing ever) I mean fun. But becouse it's an opportunity to create a world, and to tell a story.
