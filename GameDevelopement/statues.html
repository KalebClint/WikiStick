Statues - Game Jam Project

Overview

Statues was the game I made for my first ever game jam. Its a simple 3D horror game where you are lost inside a dark mansion with nothing but a torch. The mansion is full of statues, these statues will move towards you, unless you are looking at them. It was a simple game with not great graphics or even full funcionality but it was a good learning experience and a decent attempt for my first try.

As I actually quite liked the idea I decided to re-make the game for my Digitech project in 2024, this is my current in progress project.
