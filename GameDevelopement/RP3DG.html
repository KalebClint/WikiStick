Overview

What I have nicknamed the PP3DG, is able to generate a full and completely random map that is completely different every time.

How It Works

Here I will go over basically how much I understand of how it works, as so far its probably the most complex feature I had implemented into Godot.
Basically the map is made up of cells, each cell has four wals and four doors, the grid map is used to create rooms with cells together. Each cell has an index dependant on the type, say the index is 0 then the type is a room cell. or 1 = door cell. 2 = hallway cell and 3 = to a border cell. Further numbers is just for different types of rooms.
