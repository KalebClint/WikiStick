The Amulet Of Katari - Game Jam

Overview

Your village is cursed with endless rain that poisons anything it touches, only you are given and amulet from the sacred shrine that protects you. In the game you have to venture through different challenges to reach the end.

I believe this was my first game jam that I did with Godot, and also my first ever 3d platformer, not that I'd made many games by this point.
