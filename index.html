Main Point

The main point of this project, is to make use of the Feynamn technique. Basically the best way to learn something, is to try and teach it. So everything I research and learn, I'll try to 'explain,' here. That will help me to identifty the things I don't know or know less about, of which I can go and research.
It's also a nice place to just put together all of my projects and hobbies, sort of my replacement of a journal, which I never was very good at doing.
