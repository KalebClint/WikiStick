Proper Acceleraion

Proper acceleration can be considered as the act of acceleration. It can be measured currently by say an accelerometer. Say for example if you are inside a car. When you feel the acceleration of the car, that is proepr acceleration.

Coordinate Acceleration

Coordinate Accceleraion however, is when you are outisde the car. You meausre specific places of accerlation from the car. You take the time, and you take the velocity to figure out the accerlation in that specific frame of time.
